xchat-gnome Downloads

Before you download here, check your distribution. More and more distributions are including xchat-gnome in their packages, and a package will be easier than building from a source release.

As of 0.5, xchat-gnome can use libsexy for sexier behavior (such as clickable links in topics and spell-checking). Beware that if you're currently on pango 1.10.0, this will make xchat-gnome unusable, due to a bug with 1.10.0's cairo implementation. This is fixed in pango HEAD, and 1.10.1. Older (1.8.x) releases are also fine.
