Building xchat-gnome is not a super trivial operation at this point. Here are some basic instructions, but as is often the case with young projects such as this, your mileage may vary. Note that as of now, building xchat-gnome requires GTK+ version 2.3 or higher

DO NOT expect support from the normal X-Chat developers, and do not submit bug reports/feature requests/etc to them. Either email the address below or stop by #xchat-gnome on irc.freenode.net.

You can also submit bugs in the "xchat-gnome bugs" tracker at http://rt.navi.cx. Use the login "guest" with password "guest".

To build a release, just follow the normal autotools procedure. Make sure that you pass the --enable-gnomefe flag to configure.

To compile from SVN:
$ cvs -d :pserver:anonymous@cvs.sf.net:/cvsroot/xchat co xchat2
$ cd xchat2
xchat2$ svn cat http://navi.cx/svn/misc/trunk/xchat-gnome/buildsystem.diff | patch -p0
xchat2$ pushd src
src$ svn co http://navi.cx/svn/misc/trunk/xchat-gnome/fe-gnome/
src$ popd
xchat2$ ./autogen.sh
xchat2$ ./configure --enable-maintainer-mode --enable-gnomefe
(make sure that gnome support was found, and gtkspell if desired)
xchat2$ make
xchat2$ cd src/fe-gnome
xchat2$ gconftool-2 --install-schema-file=apps_xchat.schemas.in.in
fe-gnome$ ./xchat-gnome
