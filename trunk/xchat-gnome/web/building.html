Building xchat-gnome is not a super trivial operation at this point. Here are some basic instructions, but as is often the case with young projects such as this, your mileage may vary.

$ cvs -d :pserver:anonymous@cvs.sf.net:/cvsroot/xchat co xchat2
$ cd xchat2 xchat2$ svn co http://navi.cx/svn/misc/trunk/xchat-gnome/buildsystem.diff
xchat2$ patch -p1 < buildsystem.diff
xchat2$ svn co http://navi.cx/svn/misc/trunk/xchat-gnome/common.diff
xchat2$ patch -p1 < common.diff
xchat2$ pushd src
src$ svn co http://navi.cx/svn/misc/trunk/xchat-gnome/fe-gnome/
src$ popd
xchat2$ ./autogen.sh
xchat2$ ./configure --enable-maintainer-mode --enable-xft --enable-gnomefe
(make sure that gnome support was found, and gtkspell if desired)
xchat2$ make
xchat2$ cd src/fe-gnome
fe-gnome$ ./xchat-gnome
