This is the interviewee client.
It has to perform the following actions

Authentication of the interviewee.

Get Question
Answer Question
Reject Question
Quit

Scenarios: (others may call these usecases)
These are in the authentication mode
1)	Starting the interview w/ a question waiting
2)	Starting the interview w/o a question waiting

These are when the chat is in progress, and are completed with an answer, or a rejection
3)	Continuing the interview w/ a question waiting
4)	Continuing the interivew w/o a question waiting
5)	Ending the interview w/ a question waiting
6)	Ending the interview w/o a question waiting
